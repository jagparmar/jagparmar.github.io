FireSAR Research Project: February 2025 - April 2025

Conducted applied research on wildfire detection using Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) and interferometric techniques to evaluate InSAR’s potential for predicting wildfire behavior. Collected and processed Sentinel-1 datasets from ASF Vertex to generate before-, during-, and after-fire interferograms over multiple Canadian wildfire sites. Integrated ESA SNAP for interferogram creation, filtering, geocoding, and export, followed by overlay and analysis in Google Earth alongside Sentinel-2 optical imagery. Characterized wildfire perimeters, burn severity, and potential holdover fire indicators by comparing phase and intensity image signatures against optical data to validate detection accuracy.
